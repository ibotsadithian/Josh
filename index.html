Who we are?

JOSH - James old students home

JOSH, the alumni organization of St. James Matriculation Higher Secondary School, is a dynamic and thriving community that brings together graduates from the school's very first batch in 1993 to the most recent batch 2023. This organization plays a crucial role in fostering connections, preserving memories, and contributing to the continued success of the school.
